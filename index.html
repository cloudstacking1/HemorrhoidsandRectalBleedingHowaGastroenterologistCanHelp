What Is a Gastroenterologist?

A gastroenterologist is a medical doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders related to the digestive system. This includes conditions affecting the stomach, intestines, liver, pancreas, and rectum. Gastroenterologists undergo extensive training to provide expert care for issues such as acid reflux, Crohn's disease, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), ulcers, and hemorrhoids.

If you're experiencing persistent rectal bleeding, pain, or discomfort, consulting a gastroenterologist in NYC is the first step toward understanding the cause and receiving the proper treatment. Gastroenterologists are highly skilled in identifying the root causes of symptoms and can recommend treatments ranging from lifestyle changes to minimally invasive procedures.

Common Symptoms Treated by a Gastroenterologist

Hemorrhoids:

Swollen veins in the lower rectum or anus that can cause itching, pain, and bleeding.

Rectal Bleeding:

Bleeding from the anus may occur due to hemorrhoids or other gastrointestinal issues.

Painful Bowel Movements:

Pain during or after bowel movements can indicate a variety of conditions, including hemorrhoids, anal fissures, or gastrointestinal disease.

Chronic Constipation:

Persistent constipation can lead to hemorrhoids and exacerbate other gastrointestinal issues.
